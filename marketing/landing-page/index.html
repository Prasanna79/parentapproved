Single-purpose

Is this for you?

This is for you if…

  • ✓ You have an Android TV (Mi Box, Chromecast with Google TV, Shield, or any Android TV box)
  • ✓ You already know which YouTube™ playlists or channels your kids should watch
  • ✓ You’re comfortable installing an app outside the Play Store (we have a step-by-step guide)
